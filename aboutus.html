About Hare Krishna Logistics

Hare Krishna Logistics was established in the year 2014 to provide the international Air and Ocean Freight forwarding services between India to worldwide countries.

Hare Krishna Logistics is young and aggressive company offering high quality services across the globe. We offer a comprehensive suite of services extending from international freight forwarding to both origin and destination services, including freight consolidation, warehousing and distribution management. We provide door-to-door service, for both FCL and LCL load shipments to the global market. Hare Krishna Logistics offers a competitive rate structure for both air and ocean export services. We specialize in the handling of RMG, auto, pharma and marble stone. Hare Krishna Logistics has an extensive network of overseas agents to ensure point to point shipping convenience.

Our Performance measurement has been enhanced by the development of a program that compares export sailing date at the time of booking with the actual lading date. This and other similar metrics are shared with customers to raise service levels.

Both import and export airfreight services for time-sensitive shipments are available as an expedient alternative to ocean transportation. Our extensive global network enables us to efficiently coordinate documentation from departure to arrival at destination.

Hare Krishna Logistics is Head Quartered in New Delhi and additionally operates from seven different cities in India including Mumbai, Kolkata, Chennai, Bangalore.
